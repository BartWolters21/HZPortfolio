How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You can print a document by going to: >print.hz.nl
Here you should log in with your HZ account, then select a document and printer, and your document is ready to be collected at the selected printer!

How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

Go to one of the printers provided by HZ with the document that you want to scan. Put your HZ card into the slot to your right. Put your document on the scannning area. Then select 'Scan' where you just put your HZ card. And the document should get sent to your email.

What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

Get tested for covid. A self test or at a test centrum are both sufficient. Inform your teachers and stay home until you get a negative result. Incase you get a positive result, inform your teachers and listen to their instructions.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You'll need your HZ card for a start. After that you'll boot up google maps and type in: 'ZIB Investments'. Drive to there and at the gate use your HZ card at the scanner. And there you go you can now park.
